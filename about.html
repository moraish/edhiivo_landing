About Us

Who We Are

Edhivo is a small team passionate about education. We build software tools that help students learn more effectively and help educators deliver better experiences.

Our Mission

We believe the right tools can make learning stick. Our mission is to build simple, focused products that remove friction from the learning process — whether that's mastering vocabulary, taking interactive quizzes, or tracking progress over time.

What We Build

We currently offer two products:

  • The Platform — an interactive learning platform with quizzes, flashcards, and real-time analytics, built for students and educators.
  • Vocably — a GRE vocabulary app powered by spaced repetition to help students retain words long-term.

We're always working on new ideas. If you have a suggestion or want to collaborate, we'd love to hear from you.
